You received an image named evidence.jpg linked to a recent incident. Your objective is to extract hidden metadata to determine the camera model, creation time, and geolocation.

  1. Launch the VM: Click the **VM Running** button on the left to start your remote desktop session.
  2. **Transfer File:** Imagine transferring evidence.jpg to your VM.
  3. **EXIF Data Extraction:** Open a terminal in the VM and use **ExifTool** (a powerful metadata analyzer, pre-installed in Kali) to dump all information: 
    exiftool ~/Desktop/evidence.jpg
  4. **Filtered Search:** Filter the output to look specifically for camera and GPS information: 
    exiftool -Model -DateTimeOriginal -GPSPosition ~/Desktop/evidence.jpg
  5. **Geolocation Mapping:** If coordinates are found, note the Latitude and Longitude for external mapping.

Task Summary

Complete the extraction inside the VM. Identify the camera model and determine if any GPS coordinates are present in the output.
